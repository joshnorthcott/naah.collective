Zone 01 — Historic District

Centro

Where history meets high returns

Mérida's historic centre is one of the most architecturally rich neighbourhoods in Latin America. Colonial mansions, cobblestone streets, and a thriving cultural scene make Centro the first choice for lifestyle buyers, expat renovators, and short-term rental investors seeking premium Airbnb yields. Demand consistently outpaces supply as inventory of authentic colonial homes is finite.

  • Boutique rental properties targeting the growing digital nomad market
  • Long-term holds benefiting from Mérida's consistent year-on-year price appreciation
  • Mixed-use ground floor commercial + residential upper floors

Why Centro Now?

Mérida has been ranked one of the safest cities in Mexico for over a decade. As more North American and European buyers discover the city, Centro properties are being acquired faster than they come to market. Early movers in the current cycle are already seeing significant appreciation on purchases made 3–5 years ago.

Zone 02 — Northern Growth Corridor

Cholul

Affordable entry, strong appreciation ahead

Just north of Mérida's city limits, Cholul is one of the fastest-growing residential areas in the Yucatan. A wave of new residential developments, improved road infrastructure, and proximity to the city's northern commercial corridor has positioned Cholul as an ideal market for investors seeking capital appreciation at accessible price points.

The Growth Story

Mérida's population and economy have expanded significantly over the past decade, and the city's growth is visibly moving northward. Areas like Cholul that were considered far from the city just five years ago are now a short drive from major shopping centres, hospitals, and schools. Investors who enter now are positioning ahead of the next infrastructure wave.

Zone 03 — Northern Expansion

Conkal

The frontier of Mérida's northward expansion

Conkal sits at the edge of Mérida's expanding metropolitan area and represents one of the most compelling early-stage investment opportunities in the region. With large land parcels still available, new residential communities under development, and improving connectivity, Conkal attracts investors with a medium-to-long-term horizon who want maximum upside at the lowest entry cost in the market.

The Early Mover Advantage

Every established neighbourhood in Mérida's north — from Altabrisa to Santa Gertrudis Copó — was once considered "too far out." Conkal is at that inflection point today. Investors who entered those areas early captured strong returns over five-year holds. Conkal presents a comparable window of opportunity right now.
