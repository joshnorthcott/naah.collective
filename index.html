Zone 01 — Historic District
Centro
Where history meets high returns

Merida's historic centre is one of the most architecturally rich neighbourhoods in Latin America. Colonial mansions, cobblestone streets, and a thriving cultural scene make Centro the first choice for lifestyle buyers, expat renovators, and short-term rental investors seeking premium Airbnb yields. Demand consistently outpaces supply as inventory of authentic colonial homes is finite.

Why Centro Now?

Merida has been ranked one of the safest cities in Mexico for over a decade. As more North American and European buyers discover the city, Centro properties are being acquired faster than they come to market. Early movers in the current cycle are already seeing significant appreciation on purchases made 3–5 years ago.

Zone 02 — Northern Growth Corridor
Cholul
Affordable entry, strong appreciation ahead

Just north of Merida's city limits, Cholul is one of the fastest-growing residential areas in the Yucatan. A wave of new residential developments, improved road infrastructure, and proximity to the city's northern commercial corridor has positioned Cholul as an ideal market for investors seeking capital appreciation at accessible price points.

The Growth Story

Merida's population and economy have expanded significantly over the past decade, and the city's growth is visibly moving northward. Areas like Cholul that were considered far from the city just five years ago are now a short drive from major shopping centres, hospitals, and schools. Investors who enter now are positioning ahead of the next infrastructure wave.

Zone 03 — Northern Expansion
Conkal
The frontier of Merida's northward expansion

Conkal sits at the edge of Merida's expanding metropolitan area and represents one of the most compelling early-stage investment opportunities in the region. With large land parcels still available, new residential communities under development, and improving connectivity to the city, Conkal attracts investors with a medium-to-long-term horizon who want maximum upside at the lowest entry cost in the Merida market.
